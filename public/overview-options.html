The Problem

Shoppers who want to buy secondhand must open a new tab, search manually, and context-switch mid-decision — causing them to abandon sustainable intent at the peak of interest.

The Solution

Mint Condition intercepts the browsing session and surfaces secondhand eBay alternatives inside a Chrome side panel — no new tabs, no friction, no lost intent.
